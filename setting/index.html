Cadel`Kra

כל דבר, לקוח לקיצוניות, רע הוא

The year is 1356 PCA (Post Cruel Age), and the land still hasn’t recovered from the cruelty of the past. Half the continent lays unclaimed, society clinging to the edges of the land, only slowly reclaiming what once was the lands of the world’s biggest empire. City states squabble over re-inhabited land, in relative peace, but in the south-west there are rumors of bigger conflicts brewing.
