Day 1, Sunday

Today is the test day in the Wizard’s Guild, meaning all the apprentices are either showing of their work in the Guildhall, or celebrating / drowning their sorrows in Wendy’s or other similar establishments.

Spark will summon the party seemingly at random, and ask them to run an “errand” for him- to discreetly deliver a magical “thing” to the room of an apprentice wizard by the name of Bemar Zehe.
